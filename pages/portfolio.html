Scarcity, IOS app

I developed an iOS application using Swift, designed to engage users in building their own virtual zoo. The primary objective of this app was to educate students about the fundamental concept of scarcity. Within the app, users are given a designated area of land where they can strategically place carnival rides and animals. The ultimate goal of the game is to achieve a high score by making judicious choices regarding the selection and placement of various animals and attractions. These choices depend on the rarity of the items and the space required for each, thus challenging players to make resourceful decisions. Please note that the project is still a work in progress.
